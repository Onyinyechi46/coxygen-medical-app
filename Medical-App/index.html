Welcome to Medical Lending

What This App Does

This is a decentralized medical lending platform built on Cardano blockchain. It allows patients to secure credit for medical bills using smart contracts.

How It Works:

  1. Deposit Collateral - Lock ADA as your credit limit
  2. Open Medical Credit - Pay medical bills immediately
  3. Repay Later - Get your collateral back minus interest
  4. Provider Gets Paid - Medical facilities receive payment instantly

Benefits:

  • No credit checks required
  • Transparent interest rates
  • Instant provider payments
  • Secure on-chain transactions
